# voec-listing

### A Google Chrome extension that lets you check whether or not a website/company is registered under VOEC.

---

## Usage:

<img src="https://i.gyazo.com/857f8c3c8d973615f1604e263bec61e4.png" alt="drawing" width="240"/>

*An example of a company/website (Github) being listed under VOEC.*


<img src="https://i.gyazo.com/77a60479aa6491532438472c1335261f.png" alt="drawing" width="210"/>

*An example of a company/website not being listed under VOEC.*

Click the extension, and it will tell you whether or not the current tab is listed under VOEC.
